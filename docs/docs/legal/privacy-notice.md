# Privacy Notice

## 1. Who controls the data

UpdSpace currently operates under the responsibility of **Mihhail Matvejev** as an individual project operator. Questions about privacy and personal data can be sent to `privacy@updspace.com`.

## 2. What this notice covers

This notice explains how UpdSpace processes personal data when users access the platform, authenticate, participate in tenant spaces, interact with social features, connect external gaming accounts, and use related services.

## 3. Categories of data

- account and identity data received through UpdSpaceID;
- tenant profile data such as username, display name, first name, last name, and bio;
- community, team, voting, event, and achievement participation data;
- feed, news, comment, and reaction data;
- external account link data and connector-derived activity data;
- session, security, and device metadata reasonably necessary to operate the platform.

## 4. Purposes

- authenticate users and maintain sessions;
- operate tenant communities and social features;
- enforce authorization and product safety rules;
- process votes, events, achievements, and feed delivery;
- detect abuse, protect the service, and support incident response;
- comply with legal obligations and respond to verified user requests.

## 5. Legal bases

Depending on the flow, processing is based on one or more of the following:

- performance of the service requested by the user;
- legitimate interests in operating and securing the platform;
- user consent, especially for optional integrations and cookies not strictly necessary;
- compliance with legal obligations.

## 6. Sharing

Personal data is shared only with service providers and infrastructure components reasonably required to run UpdSpace, such as hosting, storage, and identity infrastructure, or where disclosure is legally required.

## 7. Retention

Retention is controlled by service-specific schedules and purge processes. The canonical retention baseline is documented in the [Retention Schedule](./retention-schedule.md).

## 8. Rights

Users may request access, export, correction, restriction, or deletion of their personal data where applicable by contacting `privacy@updspace.com`. The operational handling flow is described in the [DSAR Procedure](./dsar-procedure.md).

## 9. International processing

UpdSpace currently operates with Russian infrastructure and plans a dedicated EU deployment path. Cross-border implications are described in the [Cross-Border Transfers](./cross-border-transfers.md).

## 10. Contact

Primary contact: `privacy@updspace.com`

Postal address for formal notices is handled via validated privacy requests and should be finalized in the public launch package before broad public rollout.
