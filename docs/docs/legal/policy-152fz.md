# 152-FZ Personal Data Policy

## 1. Operator

Operator: **Mihhail Matvejev**
Contact for personal data matters: `privacy@updspace.com`

## 2. Purpose of the policy

This document sets the baseline rules for processing personal data in UpdSpace under Russian personal data requirements and is intended as the project's internal and publication-ready foundation, subject to final release packaging.

## 3. Principles of processing

- lawfulness and good faith;
- purpose limitation;
- data minimization;
- accuracy and relevance;
- storage limitation;
- protection against unauthorized or accidental access, destruction, modification, blocking, copying, provision, or dissemination.

## 4. Categories of personal data processed

- identification and account data;
- tenant profile and participation data;
- event, voting, gamification, and feed interactions;
- external connector and account-link data when the user initiates such integration;
- technical and security data required for service operation.

## 5. Processing operations

Collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification, extraction, use, transfer where necessary for service operation, anonymization where applicable, blocking, deletion, and destruction.

## 6. Security measures

- role-based access control;
- service-level segregation and separate databases;
- BFF-based browser access model with cookie sessions;
- internal request signing and context isolation;
- logging, audit, and incident handling processes;
- retention and purge procedures where implemented.

## 7. Rights of the data subject

The subject may request information about processing, correction of inaccurate data, and deletion or restriction where permitted by law. Requests are handled through `privacy@updspace.com`.

## 8. Publication note

For final public publication, the operator contact block must be supplemented with a formal service address or equivalent legally sufficient notice channel.
